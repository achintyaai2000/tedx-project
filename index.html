01 — Theme Reveal

Every idea worth spreading
started as a pivot.

TEDx6.0 asked MDI Gurgaon to change the lens. TEDx7.0 asks what happens the instant you do, the pivot itself. It's the engineer who walks into a MDI classroom for the first time. The dancer who becomes an analyst. The founder who kills the product to save the company. PIVOT is a day of six people who turned, and never went back.
